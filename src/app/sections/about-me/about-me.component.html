About me

Hello! I am a passionate Software Architect with a strong inclination towards open source, good experience in distributed systems design, and a deep love for software development.

Since the beginning of my career, I have been drawn to the world of open source, attracted by its collaborative philosophy and the freedom to explore and contribute to innovative projects.

Over time I have tackled complex challenges, architecting highly scalable and reliable distributed systems. I have a solid understanding of the fundamental principles of microservices, as well as the implementation of patterns and best practices to ensure the success of service-oriented architectures.

My journey in software development has led me to explore and master a wide range of technologies and programming languages. I am passionate about research and continuous learning, always seeking out new trends and innovations that can improve the way we build and deliver high-quality software.

My vision is to create and promote scalable, flexible, and secure systems, embracing the best solutions that open source has to offer. I firmly believe in sharing experiences and knowledge to build a stronger and more collaborative community of developers.
